Express 56 implements V.92 Technology

While the majority of providers have placed new tech­no­logy im­ple­men­tation and standards on hold due to market uncertainty, Express 56 continues to focus on end-user needs, the introduction and graceful im­ple­men­tation of new technologies to fill those needs, and increa­sing the coverage and scope of our dial-up ser­vi­ces.   • Higher speed of browsing the web, up to 300K from 56K.
  • QuickConnect allows 2x faster dial-up to get connected.
  • Higher speed of upload, from 33K up to 48K.
  • "Modem On Hold" allows receiving incoming phone calls like call-waiting, eliminating the need for a 2nd phone line.
  • V.92 is back compatible with the V.90 standard
